Frequently Asked Questions

Is this medical advice?

No. This database provides educational information only. Always consult with a healthcare professional before starting any supplement regimen.

How do you determine supplement quality?

We analyze third-party testing results, manufacturing standards (GMP certification), recall history, and transparency of sourcing. Brands are rated based on these objective criteria.

What's the difference between Simple and Advanced modes?

Simple Mode: Shows 3 top recommendations with plain language explanations. Perfect for beginners.

Advanced Mode: Shows all options with technical details, research levels, and detailed mechanisms. For experienced users.

How accurate is your interaction checker?

We use multiple databases including DrugBank and peer-reviewed research. However, always verify interactions with your healthcare provider or pharmacist.

Do you sell supplements?

No. We never sell supplements directly. We may earn small commissions from affiliate links to verified retailers, but this never influences our recommendations.

How often is the database updated?

We review new research weekly and update our database monthly. Major safety alerts are added immediately.

Can I trust your evidence ratings?

GOLD = Multiple human RCTs with consistent results

SILVER = Some human studies, promising but not definitive

BRONZE = Animal/cell studies only, limited human data

About Whole Health DB

Our Mission

We're tired of the supplement industry's bullshit. Too many sites are either selling you their own products or drowning you in scientific jargon. We built Whole Health DB to be different.

Our goal: Give you honest, evidence-based information about supplements without the sales pitch. No proprietary blends, no miracle cures, no pseudoscience.

How We're Different

  • Independent: We don't manufacture or sell supplements
  • Transparent: All our funding sources are disclosed
  • Evidence-based: Every claim is backed by research
  • Safety-first: Warnings are never hidden or minimized
  • User-focused: Built for real people, not supplement nerds

Our Team

We're a small team of researchers, developers, and former supplement industry insiders who got fed up with the status quo. No venture capital, no corporate overlords, just a commitment to helping people make informed decisions.

Our Promise
